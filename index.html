TLE Family
Weekly Live CodeClass
At TLE we provide LIVE coding classes on the most relevant CP topics. The classes are taught by people who are not just great competitive programmers themselves but also amazing instructors with years of experience in teaching.
Daily Curated Practice Problems
We also provide daily curated practice problems with dedicated video solutions, to develop your problem-solving skills and consistency. The problems are carefully selected to help you avoid redundancy and cover every topic extensively.
Instant Doubt Support
Stuck in a CP problem from or outside the course? Do not waste another second because we have got you covered with instantaneous on-chat doubt support by high-rated coders, along with dedicated on-voice live doubt resolution sessions.
Progress Tracking, Contests, and Prizes
With our Codeforces web integration, you can track your solved and unsolved problems. We also host monthly CP contests to have healthy competition among our students. Top performers also stand a chance to win TLE goodies and an offer to work with us.
Post Contest Discussions
Attempted a contest but can not up-solve on your own? That's where our PCDs come in! In these discussions for Codeforces, Codechef, and Atcoder contests you get valuable insights from our experts and learn from your mistakes to improve your performance in future contests.
Profile Review Sessions
We believe in providing our students with personalized guidance to help them achieve their goals. That's why we offer regular profile review sessions, where we analyze the Codeforces profiles, identifying areas of improvement, and providing customized feedback on how to increase your Codeforces rating.
